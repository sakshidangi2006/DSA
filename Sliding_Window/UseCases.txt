Sliding Window is a powerful technique used in various applications, such as:
- Finding the maximum sum of a subarray of a given size
- Finding the minimum window substring
- Finding the longest substring without repeating characters
- Finding the number of substrings with exactly k distinct characters
- Finding the longest subarray with a sum equal to a given value   
- Finding the longest subarray with a sum less than or equal to a given value
- Finding the longest substring with at most k distinct characters
- Finding the longest substring with at least k distinct characters
- Finding the longest substring with at least k repeating characters
- Finding the longest substring with at most k repeating characters
- Finding the longest substring with exactly k repeating characters
- Finding the longest substring with at least k distinct characters and at most m distinct characters
- Finding the longest substring with at least k repeating characters and at most m repeating characters
